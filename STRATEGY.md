# Poppy $10k Challenge — Landing Page Strategy

Target: **Bet #1** — the $400/year consumer offer, cold traffic (IG paid).

## Why this page converts cold traffic

**The angle.** Cold traffic doesn't know Poppy and doesn't care about features.
The hero sells the *pain* first ("research scattered across 14 tabs") and the
*outcome* second ("your next viral post is buried in there"). Product
explanation comes after the emotional hook.

**Structure (classic direct-response flow):**
1. Hero — pain hook + one-line product promise + CTA + trust strip (Trustpilot, 11k customers, Notion/Speechify/Whop)
2. Problem agitation — 3 specific pains of using bare ChatGPT
3. Pivot — one-sentence mechanism ("a canvas the AI can actually see")
4. How it works — 3 steps, zero jargon
5. Use cases — 6 ICP-specific jobs-to-be-done with baked-in results
6. Testimonial wall — real names, real numbers ($75k deal, 240K followers, $29k on $800 spend)
7. Pricing — cost-of-alternatives anchor ($744/yr) vs $400/yr, guarantee box, single plan card
8. FAQ — kills the top 5 objections, including "isn't this a wrapper?"
9. Final CTA — loss-framed ("every day you research the slow way…")

## Checklist vs the judging tips
- **Speak to ICP** — creators/marketers/founders named in eyebrow, use cases match their actual jobs
- **Great copy** — pain-first, specific numbers, no filler
- **Use cases** — dedicated section with results embedded
- **Mobile friendly** — fully responsive + sticky bottom CTA bar (hides when pricing card is visible)
- **Testimonials** — 7 real ones with real numbers, highlighted with marker effect
- **Benefits over features** — every feature stated as an outcome
- **Fast** — zero frameworks, ~15KB of CSS/JS, two font families, no images (product mock is pure CSS/SVG)
- **Easy to read** — 16.5px body, short paragraphs, high contrast
- **Offer display** — price anchoring, per-day framing, guarantee, single unmissable plan card

## Design (the "not AI-built" part)
- Warm paper palette + poppy-red accent (on-brand, nobody else will have it —
  every AI page is purple-gradient-on-dark)
- Fraunces serif display + Inter body — editorial, not template
- Hand-built CSS mockup of the actual canvas UI: video/PDF/voice nodes, dashed
  connectors, animated multiplayer cursors, typing AI bubble
- Slight card rotations, marker-highlight `<mark>`s, offset hard shadow on the
  price card — deliberate human touches
