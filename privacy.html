Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This page summarizes how the Poppy AI service handles your information. The complete, authoritative Privacy Policy is published at getpoppy.ai/privacy-policy. If anything on this page conflicts with that document, that document controls.

1. Information collected

  • Account details — your name, email address and login credentials.
  • Payment details — handled by a payment processor; Poppy does not store full card numbers.
  • Your content — the videos, documents, audio, links and text you add to your boards, and the outputs you generate.
  • Usage data — device, browser and interaction data used to keep the service running and improve it. This landing page also passes standard ad-attribution parameters (such as utm_*) through to checkout so paid campaigns can be measured.

2. How it’s used

  • To provide the service: content you add to a board is processed by the AI models you invoke (such as those behind ChatGPT and Claude) to generate your outputs.
  • To operate, secure and improve Poppy, and to provide customer support.
  • To send service and marketing emails, which you can opt out of at any time.

3. Sharing

Information is shared only with the service providers needed to run Poppy (hosting, AI model providers, payments, analytics, support tooling) under appropriate agreements. Your data is not sold.

4. Cookies

Cookies and similar technologies are used to keep you signed in, remember preferences and measure how the product and campaigns perform.

5. Retention and your choices

Content stays on your boards until you delete it or close your account. You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by emailing support@getpoppy.ai.

6. Children

Poppy AI is not directed at children and should not be used by anyone under the age required by the laws of their region.

7. Contact

Privacy questions? Email support@getpoppy.ai, or see the full policy at getpoppy.ai/privacy-policy.
